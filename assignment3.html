Serena Jameka Williams, born September 26th, 1981, is now a professional tennis player. Williams was born in Saginaw, Michigan and is the youngest of five daughters including her sister,Venus Williams When the children were young, the family moved to Compton, California where Williams started playing tennis at the age of 4. In addition to being a tennis superstar, Serena is a model, activist, entertainer, philanthropist and author. Williams released her first solo auto biography entilted On the Line, following the 2009 US Open.
